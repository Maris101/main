For the first 18 set the humidity to 40-50 percent, for the final days of hatching you must turn it up to 65-75 percent or the chicks won’t hatch. The normal temperature for an incubator is 99f-100f if the temperature goes above or below that mark the chicks won’t hatch so be careful and keep a close eye on it over the course of the hatching period. After the first week go into a dark room and candle the egg, or put a strong light under the egg (like a flashlight, or phone) if you see veins the egg is fertile and might hatch, if not it won’t.
